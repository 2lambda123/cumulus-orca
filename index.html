Operational Recovery Cloud Archive (ORCA)

Providing a second line of defense for your Cumulus data.

Get Started

Easy to Use

ORCA is designed to be easy to use for data recovery operations. Whenever possible, ORCA utilizes native Cumulus components and architecture for direct integration into your current Cumulus deployment strategies.

Extensible

ORCA is not a one-size-fits-all solution and was designed to be flexible and adaptable. Each DAAC can customize ORCA by extending functionalities to meet unique recovery and archive needs.

Powered by AWS

ORCA utilizes NASA NGAP approved AWS services and components to meet your team's security needs.
